About Us.

Sri Lakshmi Prasanna Aquaculture Private Limited has been established by K V Ramana Reddy in the year 1992. It was registered as a Private Limited company under The Companies Act,1956. It has been producing various species of Shrimp such as Penaeus Monodon , Scampi,Penaeus indicus and Litopenaeus Vannamei. The present Directors of the Company are D.Surendranath Reddy , K.Narendranath Reddy and K.Nishanth Reddy. The registered office of the company is at Kothakodur(V), TP Gudur(M), SPSR Nellore, Andhra Pradesh. The Company and it's subsidiaries are spread in an area of 200 Acres spread along the East Coast of India. The Company has started cultivating shrimp in the year 1989 and has been in the field for almost 3 decades.
